CLUB INFO

ACM BIT Mesra chapter is an extension of ACM, an international scientific and educational organization dedicated to advancing the art, science, engineering, and application of information technology. Our vision is to "dream, share, and innovate". We aim at creating a platform for exchange of ideas and knowledge, and promoting computing research among students. We do this by providing a learning platform with healthy competition, conducting tutorials and workshops, and organizing coding contests. Our goal is to enhance learning through exchange of ideas, and help students develop their skills and maintain a competitive advantage.
